# Projects
## Analysis of a bike sharing company
A hypothetical company is looking to convert all their customers into annual members. The business task required to understand how users without an annual membership, called *casuals*, differ from members in the way they use the services offered by the company. The goal was achieved through the analysis of historical data of the past twelve months.  
**Tools**: R
